Tsumugu

A client-side, offline comprehensible-input generator and graded reader, hooked to a persistent record of the vocabulary you've learned. Each text is generated to land at 80–95% known words, so it grows with your level.

📖 Open the reader The interactive reader — coloured by what you know, hover for definitions, grade as you go. 📚 Browse the wiki Graded readings, per-word vocab encoders, and cross-linked word atoms. ⌨︎ View the source The open engine — language-agnostic, client-side, Apache-2.0.
