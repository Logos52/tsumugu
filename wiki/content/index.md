A compounding, Karpathy-style **LLM-wiki** woven from real reading. Every word/idiom you meet becomes a durable, cross-linked page; words you're memorizing also get an **encoding-layer** page (etymology, mnemonics, "why it's tricky").

## Languages

- [[zh-Hant/夜市|中文 (Traditional)]] — Taiwan Mandarin
- vi (Tiếng Việt) — bootstrapped through Chinese via the **Hán-Việt bridge**

## How pages get here

Reading in the [Tsumugu reader](https://github.com/Logos52/tsumugu) → flag what's unclear → `pnpm gen wiki` / `pnpm gen encoding` drafts a page into `Inbox/` → clean it up → promote here. Captured web pages arrive the same way via the Obsidian Web Clipper.

> Published: general word/idiom/encoding pages. Never published: your private status, flags, word store, and custom dictionary.
