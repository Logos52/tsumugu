# 夜市

## Meaning
晚上才開的市場。可以一邊走一邊吃**小吃**、買東西、看表演，氣氛通常很**熱鬧**。台灣的夜市（士林、逢甲、六合）很有名。

> *Night market — an evening street market famous in Taiwan for cheap food, games, and a lively crowd.*

## Character breakdown
- **夜** (yè) — night. 夕 "evening" + 亦 (phonetic). Contrast 日 "day".
- **市** (shì) — market / city. A pictograph of a marketplace; also in 城市 "city", 超市 "supermarket".

## Similar / related
- [[xiaochi-snacks|小吃]] — the food you eat there.
- [[renao-lively|熱鬧]] — what a good 夜市 feels like.
- 市場 (shì chǎng) — a market in general (not necessarily at night).

## Examples (from your reading)
- 今晚我們去**夜市**吃小吃。
- 台灣的**夜市**很有名。

## Usage / register
Everyday, neutral. Count with 個: 一個夜市. The activity is 逛夜市 (guàng yè shì) "to stroll a night market".
